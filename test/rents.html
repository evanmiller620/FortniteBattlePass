What is the average rent in San Francisco?

The average rent for an apartment in San Francisco is $3,397. The cost of rent varies depending on several factors, including location, size, and quality.

What is the average apartment size in San Francisco?

The average size for a San Francisco, CA apartment is 735 square feet, but this number varies greatly depending on unit type, with cheap and luxury alternatives for houses and apartments alike. Studio apartments are the smallest and most affordable, 1-bedroom apartments are closer to the average, while 2-bedroom apartments and 3-bedroom apartments offer a more generous square footage.

What are the most affordable neighborhoods in San Francisco?

The most affordable neighborhoods in San Francisco are Tenderloin , where the average rent goes for $2,023/month, Downtown District 8 - North East , where renters pay $2,235/mo on average, and North Waterfront , where the average rent goes for $2,625/mo. If you’re looking for other great deals, check out the listings from Lower Pacific Heights ($3,003), Forest Knolls ($3,043), and Inner Mission ($3,061), where the asking prices are below the average San Francisco rent of $3,397/mo.

What are the most expensive neighborhoods in San Francisco?

The most expensive neighborhoods in San Francisco are Duboce Triangle ($4,249), Nob Hill ($4,450) and Presidio ($4,917).

What are the most popular neighborhoods in San Francisco?
